Color Scheme

I chose this color palette because I think it is bright and attractive to my target audience. It has a fresh modern appeal while applying to the name of my site with the blue for snow and the yellow for sun. I think there are good color values here to create contrast and interest.

  • The darkest Color will be for the banner, nav bar, and font.
  • The Blue will be for anywhere that I want to draw attention to.
  • The Yellow color will be the logo.
  • The Light yellow will be for details.
  • The grey will be for the background.
