    Event Hall

    Our event hall is available for reservations for special events such as wedding receptions, dinners, and group gatherings. The following is included with rental of the space:

  • Use of the food prep kitchen
  • 10 Tables
  • 80 Chairs
  • Plates
  • Cups
  • Utensils
  • Table linens
  • Event vendor List
  • Options available for catering.
  • This room can accomodate 80 guests for a sit down dinner, or 125 guests for a reception.
